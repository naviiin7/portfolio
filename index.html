About Me

Hi, I’m a developer from Kochi passionate about building fast, intuitive user experiences. I specialize in Angular, and one of my flagship projects is a fully-designed front-end e-commerce platform that reflects the design quality and functionality of modern enterprise-level shopping sites. Before tech, I worked with Amazon North America, strengthening my communication and problem-solving abilities. When I’m not coding, I’m on a football field, competing as a gamer, or cultivating plants.
